I’m most energized when I’m coding in Swift, shaping user experiences, and sharing my progress to inspire others. Recognition matters, but what drives me deeper is proving that reinvention is possible at any stage of life. I’m still learning every day, not only for myself, but to inspire others that it’s never too late to pursue what you want.

At my best, I bring resilience and grit, turning rejection into motivation and using challenges as stepping stones to growth. I lead with strategy and foresight, guiding teams with clarity while anticipating risks along the way. A growth mindset allows me to thrive when I step beyond my comfort zone, while visibility and influence help me inspire others by showing what’s possible. With the courage to reinvent myself, I’ve learned that no background defines one’s ceiling, and I use my own career-switch journey as a source of inspiration to encourage others to believe in their potential.

I believe in persistence, smart work, and the courage to step into the unknown. Let’s connect if you share the same energy for building, leading, and inspiring through technology.
